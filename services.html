ACCURACY PROTOCOL

HOW I KILL HALLUCINATIONS.

STEP 01

Full-text case download

When a defendant’s brief comes in, I identify every cite, download all of them in full text, and build the argument off the actual opinions — not a legal research service’s summary, not the web, not a model’s memory.

STEP 02

Cross-model verification

Every citation and every quote is checked within the model, across other models, and against the source text. Discrepancies are flagged before a human sees the draft.

STEP 03

Human spot-check

I confirm the check personally. Record cites tied to transcript pages, case quotes against the opinion, procedural posture confirmed.

STEP 04

Advocacy-level report

Every case is scored for how literally I’m reading it. On all fours, extended by inference, or pushed into new territory. You choose the level of advocacy. I mark the line.

THE LINE I DON’T CROSS

I don’t cite cases for propositions that don’t exist. I don’t use hallucinated cases. Credibility is the product.
